## Recipe for si mple sourdough bread

### Ingredients
- 540g white flour
- 60g whole wheat/rye flour
- 10g salt
- 335g cold water 
- 120g sourdough starter

### Instructions
- mix all ingredients until a rough dough forms
- leave covered for 20 minutes
- knead for 5 minutes until smooth
- leave to rise until doubled in size (8 hours or overnight, covered airtight)
- shape into boule or long loaf shape and place in proofing basket
- proof for about 2 hours
- preheat oven to maximum / 300 degrees Celsius
- place bread in oven (using a dutch oven or other covered dish), turn down heat to 200 C
- bake for 30 minutes
- remove lid, bake for additional 10 to 15 minutes
